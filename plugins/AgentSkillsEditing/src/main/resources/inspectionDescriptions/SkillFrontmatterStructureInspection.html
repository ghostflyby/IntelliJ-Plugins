Reports structural issues in SKILL.md YAML frontmatter.

Validates the basic structure of the YAML frontmatter block, including the presence of --- delimiters, valid YAML syntax, that the top-level value is a mapping, and that no unknown top-level fields are present.

This inspection only checks the structural validity of the frontmatter. Field-specific validations are handled by separate inspections: SkillNameInspection, SkillDescriptionInspection, and SkillOptionalFieldsInspection.
